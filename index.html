About Us

Mygrace Photography was founded in 2019. It is regarded as one of the top photo studio in Adjumani district, uganda due to their high-quality services and professional photographers. It caters to a wide range of occasions, including graduations, maternity, newborns, and even business celebrations.

Moreover, MyGrace is also well-known for its awards, both locally and globally. Since its inception, the studio has proved that it is unrivalled in terms of both quality and speed. Their experience dates back to 2019 and they have already managed over 2000 projects. Because of their years of experience, you’ll be in good hands.

Mygrace Photography has also expanded into commercial photography, shooting photographs for advertising and marketing firms. Its photographers have extensive expertise in all types of studio photography, so you can be certain that you’re in good hands. They are mostly popular for studio pregnancy photoshoots and studio maternity photoshoots.

Mygrace photography has many branches in different number of places, Many customers can afford the prices, and the studio is open all the time. Parking for our clients is also available at no additional charge. MyGrace is well-known for a number of sources aside from its capacity to perform both photography and videography projects.

how long should i wait to received my photos?

Ans. all our works are express, you take photo, we work on it and you go with yours, unless they are many photos from 11 and above or at busy hours like X-mas time, occasions and new year photos for those, we can schedule with customers.

Do I need to make an appointment?

Ans. Yes, it's advisable to make an appointment, for a studio to in thier scheduled prior to convenient times and dates.

Do you alter pictures?

Ans. Yes, all the reputed studios provide their customers with photo retouching and photo editing services. And, we use the software named adobe photoshop and use different methods like shadow creation, image masking, etc to fix your photo according to what suit you.

Ans. A studio session might take anything from 60 minutes to an hour. it also Depends with nature of photos or video you are shooting.
